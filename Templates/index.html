About Us

Delivering Real-Time Fraud Detection Using AI and Machine Learning

We use advanced AI and Machine Learning to detect credit card fraud instantly, ensuring secure and seamless transactions. Our real-time fraud detection system helps prevent financial losses and enhances user trust.

The Problem: A busy woman named Priya got a call from her bank—her credit card was used for a $5,000 purchase in another country. She never left home. The fraud happened because banks detect scams too late.

Solution: CardShield uses AI-ML to scan every transaction in real-time. When a thief tried using Priya’s card again, CardShield blocked it instantly—before she even got a notification. No more fraud, just safe spending.

Key Message: "Stop fraud before it happens—not after."
